TextCash

TEXTCASH IN SOUTH AFRICA

TextCash allows Bank of the Sun customers to send money instantly to anyone with a valid South African cellphone number.
Instant money sent through our TextCash platform ranges from a minimum of R50 to a maximum of R1500.

How It Works..

The Bank Of the Sun

  1. Send money from your Bank of the Sun account
  2. Money is transferred securely and immediately
  3. Recipient receives an SMS with a reference pin valid for 12 hours after receiving money into the TextCash network.
